~ ABOUT US ~

With an insatiable case of wanderlust, never calling one place home, I work as a photographer, travel writer and social media consultant all over the world.

A Francophile at heart, I had long dreamed of living in France. In a twist of fate I moved to Paris to study my degree at the University of London in Paris and lived and loved in the city for three blissful years. Of all the cities I’ve visited, Paris remains closest to my heart. I would spend my days in art galleries, picnicking along the banks of the Seine, getting lost down cobbled back streets and taking day trips to nearby castles.

Since then, I have spent time exploring every corner of Europe, from the fairytale landscape of Cappadocia to the rugged coves and turquoise waters of Croatia.

Now I’m on the road again destined for foreign continents with an antipodean desire to explore further afield. I have tried the 9 to 5 for a couple of years, but my feet are too restless, my mind too flighty and my dreams too big, so I am bound for adventure once more.

If there is one thing I hope this website inspires in you, it is to travel. Keep your head in the clouds and your feet on the ground. There is a wonderful quote in an Education “Actions, character, my English teacher says, I think it means that if we never did anything, we wouldn’t be anybody.” For we are nothing but the experiences we make and the stories we share. So go forth, travel, explore the world and collect stories along the way. If you need me, I’ll be with you every step of the way.
